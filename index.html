About Mdadila Investment

Welcome to Mdadila Investment.

Mdadila Investment Co. Ltd was established to carry extraction and processing of minerals and also community centered health services.

Mdadila Investment Co Ltd is located in Nyaruyeye village in Geita region. A region well known for its rich gold deposits and active small-scale and large-scale mining operations.

The company presence in the region has contributed to local economic development through mining operations and employment opportunities.
